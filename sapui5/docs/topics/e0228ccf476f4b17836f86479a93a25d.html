Sharing ALP

You can share your ALP application for quick and easy accessibility.

You can share through:
  • Email (application URL)
  • SAP Jam (if integration with SAP Jam is configured)
  • SAP Fiori Launchpad as a tile
To share ALP, choose the icon in the title area and choose an option.
