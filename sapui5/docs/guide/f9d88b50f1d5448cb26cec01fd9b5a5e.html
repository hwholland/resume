Sharing Overview Pages

Share your application as a tile on the SAP Fiori launchpad or by sending an email link.

SAP Fiori Launchpad

The current state of the filter bar in your application is stored as a tile on the SAP Fiori launchpad.

To save the application, click Share > Save As Tile > Fill required fields > Ok

Email

You can also share the current state of filter bar by email, which contains a link to your application.

To send an email link, click Share > Send Email
