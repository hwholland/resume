Visual Business AnalyticMap Sample

  • Testing the Visual Business AnalyticMap Control.
  • The colored countries are active. They react on hover and can be selected. They raise click and (de)select events.
  • The grey countries are in-active. They do not react on hover, except pointer and tooltip, and can not be selected. They only raise click events.
  • A click on a grey country may deselect a colored country.
  • If any country is selected all other countries get a lighter color.
  • Hovered and selected countries get a dark grey border.
  • There are click and contextMenu events assigned to the map, regions, and legend entries
