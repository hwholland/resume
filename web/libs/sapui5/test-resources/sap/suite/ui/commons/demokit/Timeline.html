Timeline

Introduction

The Timeline is a control to display entries (objects/events) in their chronological order.
You can use the Timeline to provide information about changes of an object or events related to an object. The entries can be system generated (e.g. last changed by xy) or manual entries.

The control consists of:
- a title
- a chronological axis
- entries

Title
The title describes what is displayed along the timelines axis.

Axis
Along the axis the entries are arranged by their chronological order.
The Timeline can be scrolled along its axis depending on the orientation of the axis.
The default sorting of the Timeline is latest on top and the original entry as the first one (on bottom).

Entry
The entries of the timeline can be system generated entries (e.g. last changed by xy) or manual ones.
The entry highlights who changed what and when.
The entry has these sections:
- Node: You can display icons to represent the different entry types, such as comment, note, etc.
- Header: Contains an image or icon, texts and links, and timestamp
- Content: Contains texts or links, structured or unstructured information, and images
