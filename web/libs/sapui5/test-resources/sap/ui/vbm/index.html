Samples of build in Visual Objects

  • Spots This sample shows spot objects. Those are images placed a a given position.
  • Spots with Icon or Text This sample shows spot objects with icon or text.
  • Routes
    • Routes and Arrows This sample shows polyline objects, which can be used for simple arrows or complex routes.
    • Routes and Arrows with triangles in between This sample shows routes with triangles in between showing the direction of the routes.
    • Routes and Arrows with borders This sample shows Routes and Arrows with configurable border colors.
    • Routes around the World This sample shows round world capabilities.
  • Areas
    • Areas This sample shows polygon objects.
    • Multi Areas This sample shows multi polygon objects.
    • Multi Areas with exclusions This sample shows multi polygon objects with exclusions (holes).
  • Circles This sample shows circle objects. Circles can have a radius based on pixels or based on actual meters. The first ones have a constant size, thre latter ones depend on the zoom.
  • Pies and Blocks/Boxes This sample shows also a simple pie chart and a box.
